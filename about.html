About me

Growing up in New York, I began making things with my hands at a young age. Whether it was leatherworking or woodworking, something about the craftsmanship of working with a raw material and creating a product from scratch appealed to me. When I launched my career as a software engineer in 2021, I wanted to retain that same feeling when working with code.

I worked to hone my eye for design in my spare time, learning as much new tech as I could. I began working with 3D modeling software to render my design ideas, all the while thinking about how I could apply my background to web design. The result of all my tinkering is that I've grown my ability to create elegent, creative, and informative websites that portray a feeling of quality craftsmanship and artistic prowess.
